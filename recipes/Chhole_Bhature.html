Chhole Bhature

Description:

Chhole Bhature is a popular North Indian dish consisting of spicy chickpea curry (chhole) served with fluffy deep-fried bread (bhature). It is often accompanied by pickles, yogurt, and onions. This flavorful and indulgent dish is loved for its rich flavors and satisfying texture.

Ingredients:

  • Garam masala
  • All-purpose flour (maida)
  • Turmeric powder
  • Baking soda or yeast
  • Oil for frying
  • Salt
