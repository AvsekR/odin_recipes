Dal Makhni

Description:

Dal Makhani is a creamy Indian dish made with black lentils and kidney beans, cooked in a tomato-based gravy with spices and served with naan or rice.

Ingredients:

  • Black Lentils (Urad Dal)
  • Red Kidney Beans (Rajma)
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Butter or Ghee
  • Cream

Steps:

  1. Soak urad dal and rajma overnight.
  2. Boil them in water until soft.
  3. Add tomatoes, cook until soft.
  4. Stir in boiled dal and rajma, simmer.
  5. Finish with cream, simmer for a few minutes.
