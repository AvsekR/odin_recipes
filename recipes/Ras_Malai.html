Ras Malai

Description:

Ras Malai is a delicate Indian dessert made from soft, spongy cheese dumplings soaked in sweetened, thickened milk flavored with cardamom, saffron, and sometimes rose water. It's garnished with nuts like pistachios or almonds and served chilled, offering a rich and creamy indulgence.

Ingredients:

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)
  • Milk
  • Sugar
  • Cardamom
  • Saffron
  • Nuts (such as pistachios or almonds)

Steps:

  1. Make paneer by curdling milk, draining, and pressing.
  2. Boil milk, add sugar, cardamom, and saffron for flavor.
  3. Shape paneer into small dumplings.
  4. Drop dumplings into simmering milk, cook until they float.
  5. Chill in the refrigerator to set.
  6. Garnish with chopped nuts before serving.
